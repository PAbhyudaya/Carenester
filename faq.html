General Questions

What is Carenester?

Carenester is a holistic care service platform designed to provide comprehensive support for elderly and geriatric patients. We offer a wide range of services including trained caregivers, home nursing, pharmacy deliveries, physician visits, transportation, medical accessories, logistics support, and porter services.

How do I start using Carenester services?

You can start by visiting our Contact Us page to reach out to our team, or directly book a service through our Book a Service page. One of our care coordinators will get in touch with you to understand your specific needs and recommend the most suitable services.

What qualifications do your caregivers and nurses have?

All our caregivers undergo thorough background checks and specialized training in elderly care. Our nurses are licensed professionals with relevant qualifications and experience in geriatric care, critical care, or specialized nursing. We ensure all our staff members are well-trained, compassionate, and dedicated to providing the highest quality of care.

Can I switch or combine different services?

Yes, our services are designed to be flexible and can be combined to create a comprehensive care plan tailored to your specific needs. You can also modify your service package as your requirements change over time. Our care coordinators will help you determine the best combination of services for your situation.

Services Questions

What does your caregiver service include?

Our caregiver service includes personal care assistance (bathing, grooming, dressing), medication reminders, specialized dementia care, meal preparation, companionship, light housekeeping, and mobility assistance. We tailor these services based on the individual needs of each client.

How is home nursing different from caregiver service?

While caregivers focus on non-medical assistance and personal care, our home nursing service provides professional medical care including post-surgical care, wound dressing, administering injections, IV therapy, vitals monitoring, diabetic care, and respiratory management. Nurses have medical training and can perform clinical procedures that caregivers cannot.

Do you provide medical equipment for long-term use?

Yes, through our Medical Accessories service, we offer both rental and purchase options for various medical equipment including hospital beds, oxygen concentrators, wheelchairs, walkers, patient lifts, and bathroom safety equipment. We can advise on the most cost-effective option based on your expected duration of use.

What types of transportation services do you offer?

Our transportation services include non-emergency ambulance services, wheelchair-accessible cabs, regular appointment transportation, inter-facility transfers, stretcher transportation, and medical escort services. All our vehicles are equipped to ensure safe and comfortable travel for elderly and patients with mobility challenges.

Pricing Questions

How much do your services cost?

Our service pricing varies depending on the type of service, duration, frequency, and specific requirements. We offer hourly, daily, weekly, and monthly packages with different pricing tiers. Please contact our team for a detailed quote based on your specific needs.

Do you offer any discounts for long-term service commitments?

Yes, we offer discounted rates for long-term service commitments. We also have special packages for clients who require multiple services, providing cost savings compared to individual service bookings. Our care coordinators can help you identify the most cost-effective options for your needs.

Is there a cancellation fee?

We understand that plans can change. For most services, cancellations made at least 24 hours in advance do not incur any fees. However, late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a cancellation fee, typically 50% of the service cost. Emergency cancellations are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and cash. For recurring services, we offer the option of setting up automatic payments. We provide detailed invoices for all services rendered, which can be useful for insurance reimbursement purposes.

Coverage Questions

Which cities do you currently serve?

Currently, we provide our services in major metropolitan areas including Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore. We are continuously expanding our coverage to new areas. If your location is not currently covered, please contact us, and we'll let you know about our expansion plans.

Do you serve remote or suburban areas?

We primarily serve urban and suburban areas within and around our operational cities. Service availability in remote areas depends on various factors including distance, accessibility, and staff availability. Additional charges may apply for services in distant locations. Please contact us to check if your specific location is covered.

Can I use your services while traveling to a different city?

If you're traveling to one of our operational cities, you can use our services there. However, we recommend booking in advance to ensure availability. For regular clients who travel frequently, we can create a coordinated care plan across multiple cities where we operate.

Is there a minimum service area coverage?

There is no minimum service area, but availability and pricing may vary based on your specific location. Our goal is to provide services to anyone who needs them within our operational cities, regardless of the exact neighborhood or district.

Emergency Support Questions

Do you provide emergency services?

While we do offer emergency response through our ambulance service and can provide urgent care in many situations, we are not a substitute for emergency medical services (EMS). For life-threatening emergencies, we recommend calling your local emergency number first, then contacting us for follow-up care.

What is your response time for urgent requests?

Our response time for urgent requests varies depending on the service requested and your location. For ambulance services, we typically aim to arrive within 15-30 minutes. For urgent nursing or caregiver needs, we strive to provide service within 2-4 hours. During peak times or in remote areas, response times may be longer.

Is there 24/7 support available?

Yes, our customer support team is available 24/7 to handle inquiries and emergency requests. Many of our services, including home nursing and caregiver services, can be arranged on a 24/7 basis. For after-hours support, please call our emergency helpline at +91 999 888 7777.

What should I do if there's an emergency with a patient receiving your care?

If there's a medical emergency while a patient is under our care, our staff are trained to assess the situation and take appropriate action. This may include administering first aid, contacting emergency services, and notifying family members. All our caregivers and nurses are trained in basic life support and emergency response protocols.
